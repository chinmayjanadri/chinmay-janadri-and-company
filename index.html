About Us

We are a Chartered Accountants with team of qualified and motivated professionals, offering integrated one-stop services. Our primarily focus on Audit & Assurance, Management Advisory, Consultation in Taxation, Economic and Other related laws and Transaction Advisory Services.

We have exceptional and leading edge expertise in the areas of Direct Taxation, International Taxation, Goods & Services Tax, Corporate Laws, Internal Audit, Statutory Audit, Stock Audit, Implementation of Accounting Software, Implementation of IND AS, Due Diligence, Forensic and Regulatory Compliance.

From the experience gained by the founding partner, it is understood that there is a huge vacuum in the Indian professional services sector, where the firms have expertise in independent domains but are not equipped with a holistic approach to assist the client’s business. The genesis of our firm is to fill this vacuum and provide integrated solution to client’s business.

To withstand the ever-increasing competition faced by corporates in the vibrant global economy coupled with increasing responsibilities on human resources, we aim at enhancing the economic well-being of an organization by providing timely services which add significant value addition to the client’s business.

Services

At Chinmay Janadri & Co. in India, we offer a comprehensive range of audit and assurance services. Our commitment to delivering independent and unbiased opinions on the accuracy and completeness of financial statements for a given period has been established over decades.

We understand the importance of specialized teams for statutory attest functions under the Indian Companies Act and Income tax regulations. In an increasingly globalized world, US GAAP and IFRS restatements are becoming more critical, and our team has the expertise to handle both. We are also knowledgeable in Ind-As restatements.

Our approach to internal audits combines compliance audits, process adherence, data analytics, systems audits, and proprietary audits. We believe in a business approach to internal audits, and our unique representations in internal audit reports deliver value in every sphere of business.

In addition to our audit and assurance services, we specialize in management assurance and risk review, corporate governance, internal control design and review, and IFC process and walkthroughs. Our firm boasts partners with decades of experience, AI and data scientists to help gather domain knowledge, and industry domain experts in property, retail segments, and software.
